shell-less northeast egypt's armadillo nail soup

beautiful homemade food made from specific parts of everyone's favorite armadillo, a real delicacy.

ingredients

  • the nails of an armadillo found in the outskirts of the egypt's northeast
  • sea salt to taste
  • two moldy garlic cloves
  • one slice of chorizo
  • three bigmacs
  • an old dusty beer (preferably one found in an attic)
  • one healthy carrot

steps

  1. throw salt, garlic cloves, carrot, one bigmac and the slices of chorizo on a pressure cooker
  2. in the cooker put two liters of sparkling water (not tap water, we trying to be healthy here)
  3. on a plate, place the nails and pour the beer over them
  4. grate the other bigmac over the nail, and gently stir with the carrot
  5. once done, throw the nails in the pressure cooker, and leave it there for about a hour
  6. think about your life in the shower meanwhile
  7. once ready, don't let it get cold, eat as soon as possible
