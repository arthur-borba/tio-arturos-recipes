himalayan blonde bull magical testicles

delicious bull testicles, carefully and cautiously removed from the taurus blondis specimen from the Himalayan peaks.
Baked in the oven and then fried in the flames of old burnt books

ingredients

  • two himalayan blond bull testicles
  • a butter of bar
  • sea salt to taste
  • one gram of hydrogenated blue powder from the coal mines of southern Helsinki
  • two grams of Norwegian magician's booger with rhinitis
  • a clove of garlic
  • half onion

steps

  1. grate the onion and garlic clove
  2. put the bar of butter in the already hot frying pan
  3. as soon as the butter melts, put the testicles
  4. fry until golden brown
  5. throw the onion, garlic clove, booger, salt and powder in a pan with water
  6. stir with a wooden spoon over low heat until a homogeneous mixture is obtained.
  7. put the testicles on the plate, and pour the sauce on top
  8. wait until a mysterious potent force and a light blue aura consume the testicles
  9. if you want, add grated cheese to taste.
